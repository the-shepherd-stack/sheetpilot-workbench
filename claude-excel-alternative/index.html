Worked example

A shared invoice tracker needs a June West paid-total formula checked before it is copied into a report.

=SUMIFS($D$2:$D$500,$B$2:$B$500,"West",$C$2:$C$500,"Paid",$A$2:$A$500,">="&DATE(2026,6,1),$A$2:$A$500,"<"&DATE(2026,7,1))

The formula totals Amount values only when Region is West, Status is Paid, and Invoice Date falls inside June 2026. The checks tell you to confirm every SUMIFS range covers the same rows and that the date column contains real dates.
